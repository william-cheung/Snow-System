   Control Keys: W          - View moves forward
                 S          - View moves backward
                 A          - View strafes left
                 D          - View strafes Right
                 Left Mouse - Perform looking
                 Mouse      - Look about the scene
                 R          - View moves up
                 F          - View moves down
                 Up         - View pitches down
                 Down       - View pitches up
                 Left       - View yaws left
                 Right      - View yaws right
                 X          - Free the camera if it is orbiting a snowman
